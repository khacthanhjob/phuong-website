The Hand Behind the Canvas

Based in the quiet landscapes of Northern Europe, my work bridges the gap between architectural rigidity and the fluidity of nature.

Each piece is a multi-layered exploration of memory, utilizing reclaimed pigments and traditional oils to build textures that demand physical presence. My journey as the sole creator of these works is an ongoing dialogue between internal stillness and external expression.
