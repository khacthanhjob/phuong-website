The Work

An exploration of solitude and the quiet permanence of the natural world. "The Silent Watcher" utilizes layered glazes to create a sense of depth and atmospheric perspective that shifts as the viewer moves across the canvas.

Artist Statement
"I am interested in the moment before a storm breaks—the heavy, expectant air and the way light transforms a familiar landscape into something otherworldly."
Materiality

Mastery of Linen & Oil

Every piece is crafted using archival-grade Belgian linen and hand-ground pigments. The layering process involves up to twenty individual glazes, each requiring days of curing to achieve the final luminous quality.
